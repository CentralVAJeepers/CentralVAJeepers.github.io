Rules

  • Membership is by invitation only! If you have any interest in joining please PM an Admin with your info. Potential members must meet all of the following rules
  • You must be willing to participate in club organized events
  • You must support the unification of the club and represent a dignified organization
  • Members must have a decal present on their Jeep(many sizes available): it can be located anywhere including under the hood, but be mindful that by displaying the decal you are representing CVJ
  • Show respect to other members as well as other enthusiasts. No trash talking, belittling, or disrespecting any person or their Jeep(Friendly banter is ok)
  • Membership is a privilege. Many events and functions CVJ represents have others involved such as shops and other companies so please be respectful the them and the communities we are involved with
  • All members are asked to take place in at least 2 club activities per year including but not limited to trail rides, shows, meet-N-greets, and occasional meetings
  • There will be Zero tolerance for any illegal activities including illegal 4 wheeling!
  • Must have a facebook and accept friend request from the admins so we can keep in touch
  • All events posted by members are not official CVJ events unless there is an Admin involved so please respect the club when out on the trails/streets!
  • Post from he CVJ members Facebook page are not ot be shared to other pages unless permission is given by the admins... please try to keep our conversations private
  • CVJ is not responsible for you or your personal property
  • All Jeep branded vehicles welcome!
  • Be safe and have fun!
  • If your not sure of any of the above rules you can contact an admin for clarification
